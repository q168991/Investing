There are three ways of investing in Stock markets.
[1] Direct Investment - You purchase stocks in the stock market at low price and sell them at high price - Short Term/Medium/Long Term (or) you trade Futures & Options contract with the help of Technical analysis - This would be short term trading
[2] Indirect Investment Type 1 - You purchase Equity Mutual fund units. The mutual fund will invest in stock market on behalf of you.
[3] Indirect Investment Type 2 - You purchase Unit linked Insurance products operated by Insurance companies. Besides covering for your life, these products will put your money in stock markets for appreciation.

For a new comer, I would suggest an SIP in Mutual funds.
