How to start investing in share market? Please suggest. I want to do it seriously.?

Hi there!! I have left my job.. but i want 2 earn.. can investing in share market give me day today earning? can i start with a small amt like 10000 Rs. Pls suggest i seriously want 2 get into this..

It seems like you want to become a day trader. Day trading is a very difficult and very, very stressful job. It is by no means 'free' or 'easy' money. In all honesty, you would be better off at your old job, as it least offers a guaranteed income.

Investing in share market can get you a very good amount of money provided you do it sensibly. If you do it under pressure that your earnings depend on it you might take some wrong emotional decisions. I believe in investing with a goal of say 20% compounded growth each year. Anything above that is welcome. For this purpose invest in companies with strong fundamentals and at the right time buy and sell the stocks. You must study a lot and have a virtual portfolio before you put your money into it. If you spend some time investing you wil increase your base capital and then probably you will get enough to live on. Trading as a profession is dangerous if your investment gets wiped out you will have no income left.

Never buy single stocks, that is, stocks in a single company. There is no difference between single stocks and gambling. Invest in mutual funds or money markets. They have averaged 10% + over the last 70 years with much less risk than traditional stocks.

What you are talking about is active trading.

If the stock market is in a stage of momentum trading with high volumes it is possible to do this, but it like going to Las Vegas casinos to make money. Most likely, unless you are a genius (or you have insider information which is illegal) you will fail and lose money instead.

It is possible to make money through active trading to take advantage of very small market inefficiencies, but that would require large sums and money with leverage. This is what hedge funds do. That is way outside of your range.

I would strongly suggest that you look for another way of making money unless you have friends in very high positions in big global corporations.

Stocks (share market) should not be used to supplement your monthy salary. If you are out of a job, I strongly recommend holding off on the share market. In difficult times, cash is king and should be treated as such. Your efforts should be concentrated on looking for a new job and having enough cash on hand until you find a new job.
