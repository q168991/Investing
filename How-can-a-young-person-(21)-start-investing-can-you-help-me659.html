You can open a brokerage account and put your money there instead of in the savings account. While the money sits in your brokerage it also earns interest, like the savings. However in the brokerage account you'll have the ability to make some extra profits by executing winning trades when you spot a good opportunity.

Since you are on a small budget, I would suggest investing in an index fund or ETF so you are diversified in more than one or two stocks.

Do automatic deposit into your brokerage every month and build your position. Once I get up to about 10,000 I might find myself 3 stocks and spread my money amongst them.
