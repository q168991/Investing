If you want to invest money for more than 5 years, then stocks are probably a good choice. You would eant to invest in a good quality mutual fund with a long-term track record. Idealy, you would invest a steady amount every month (dollar cost averaging).

Unless you have a minimum of $1,000 (and $5,000 is more reasonable) then you should probably stay away from stocks.

You should talk to a professional financial adviser, either through your bank or a firm like Edward Jones (I don't know if they exist in the US or not...)
