In general, if you wait long enough and have a well diversified portfolio, you will make money. The idea isn't to double you money overnight, but historically, it's possible to double every 10 years or so.

Consider this: you have $10,000. You invest it as a 25 year old. It doubles every 10 years. At 35, you have $20,000. At 45, you have $20,000. At 55, you have $40,000. And, when you're thinking of retiring at 65, you have $80,000. And that assumes that you didn't put more money in along the way.

If you need to make quick money, the stock market is a risky place to do it.
