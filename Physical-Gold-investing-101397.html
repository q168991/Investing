1 oz. gold bullion coins are the U.S. Eagle, U.S. Buffalo, Canadian Maple Leaf and S.A. Krugerrand. All of these coins are recognized worldwide for maintaining the highest purity standards, and all contain exactly 1 troy ounce of pure gold. Of these only the Canadian Maple Leaf is pure 24K gold. The others (including the U.S. coins) are 22K, including a copper alloy to improve mechanical strength and handling characteristics of the coins. But all the above coins contain exactly one troy ounce of pure gold.

Buy these from a reputable gold bullion dealer such as Kitco.
