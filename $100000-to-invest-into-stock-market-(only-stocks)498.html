You will need to setup an account w/a broker to trade stocks, bonds, or mutual funds. There are several online brokerages, the largest of which are: TDAmeritrade, eTrade, Scottrade, ING direct (Sharebuilder), Charles Schwab, & Fidelity. All of them charge fees to buy & to sell stocks. Some of the fees are a flat rate, regardless of the # of shares you buy/sell, others will prorate their fees based on # of shares/total amount. Plan on paying $7-$10 per transaction. You should try to keep your fees below 5% (e.g. plan on buying at least $400 worth of stock if your fees are $10/trans). Knowing what to purchase is the really tough part. You might want to check out "The Motley Fool" or "SmartMoney" for ideas.
