GIM This is a closed end fund that invests in foreign government debt. The hypothesis here is as the U S continues to cut interest rates, foreign soveriegn debt become more valuable. Also as the value of the dollar continues to drop, assets denominated in foreign currencies also become more valuable. This pays a dividend monthly. Be sure to take the dividend into account when calculating your return.

ETP This is a pipeline limited partnership. Its value is somewhat cushioned by the high dividend payment. It has also been trending up recently as alternate sources of income have deminished greatly.
