Alex and Christine want to purchase a house. Suppose they invest 500 dollars per month into a mutual fund. How much will they have for a downpayment after 6 years if the per annum rate of return of the mutual fund is assumed to be 10.5 percent compounded monthly?

The answer will vary depending on whether they make the first payment one month from now, or immediately.
For one month from now use FVoa (Future Value ordinary annuity) = PMT [((1 + i)^n - 1) / i], for immediately, use the same times (1+i) (FVannuity due)
where n = 6yrs * 12 mos = 72, i = 0.105/12 = 0.00875
FVoa...
= 500 [(1.008575^72 - 1) / 0.00875
= 500 (99.711137)
= 49,855.57
FVad...
=49,855.57(1.00875)
=50,291.80
