It means gambling on Stocks or mutual funds.
You buy shares (part of a company) for a price and hope in time the price increases.
Say you buy shares in Yahoo. It costs $16 a share. You gamble that in 6 to 12 months the value increases to $36 a share. Then you sell, giving you a profit of $20 a share.
The gamble is the value could go down instead of up and the share is now worth $10 thus you lost $6 a share. Tell Mom that before she invests she and you pick a few companies, check the price of a share in each company. Keep a written list and every day you check online to see if the value increased. This will familiarize you with investing and not cost you a penny. Once the two of you are confident and understand what you are doing then mom invests the money
Your library should have the book "Investing For Dummies" so borrow it and the two of you read it and learn
