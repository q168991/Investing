In almost all the books I read about investments, authors, who are very successful and disciplined traders, have made it clear, that borrowing money to make investments is NOT RECOMMENDED.
You can read very very famous books in the world of investment to get sure of that such as :
Technical analysis of the financial market "Murphy"
Trading for a Living " Dr. A. Elder"
Mastering the Trade "Carter"
Trading in the Zone " Douglas"
How to make money in stocks " O'neil"
and ma nay many others. One of the reasons these traders mentioned "and they tried it and failed" is that investing what you don't own provoke psychological problems "fear for example", which could facilitates meeting wrong decisions.
If you want to read more about that, pick one of those books "or others" and read first.
Anyway, if you insist, I would never recommend you to start with Gold, silver, oil, currency or any of the 24 hours markets , since these markets are usually the most difficult. Start with something easy, like stocks .
