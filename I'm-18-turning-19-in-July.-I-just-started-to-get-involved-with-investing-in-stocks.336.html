The Stock Market is indeed a vey dangerous game of gain and loss. If you are not familiarized with the Market and intend to gain $1000.00 a day I deeply suggest you take it slow and do a vast amount of research on the prospective companies you intend to invest with. As a rule, I never invest more than half my capital. If invested wisely, which I prefer short-term, your investment should rise throughout the day: typically but not guaranteed. Once your money has doubled or the day has finished, place it in a mutual fund or some other account you prefer for storage. The next business day, reinvest up to half your new capital. Eventually you will be making over $1000.00 a day. This is not a secure or guaranteed system and should only be attempted by seasoned investors. If in the case you lose your entire investment for the day, you will still have half your capital stored away in safety and untouched. This way, you will be capable of reinvesting in order build up once again. I do not suggest you go out and jump knee deep into the Market, but DO YOUR RESEARCH. Follow up with businesses. Ask others how they invest. Learn. In time you will learn to recognize the waves of the Market and know when to invest or pull out. Be careful no matter which avenue you choose.
