Question about choosing Investing Stock Markets?

If you had $10,000 and you chose to invest in the stock market, which stocks will you invest in? were your invensterments short or long term and why did you choose these would you continue investing in these companies? what price would you sell at?

which stocks is a personal preference. well established companies with high market capitalization will usually be safer investments (for example, coca-cola or apple) and small companies with low market cap are usually more risky. stocks trading under 5$ per share are often referred to as penny stocks and are usually avoided by novice investors due to their volatility and risk.

long term vs short term really depends on your situation.
for example, a person planning on buying a house would often need cash to make a down payment on a mortgage, they would go for short term investments.
someone that already has a house might go for some more long term stuff.

as for what price, you have to sell at a higher price than the price you paid in order to make a profit (don't forget to take into account the brokerage fees for the buy and the sell transactions!)

if your stock is tanking and you want to cut your losses you may end up selling at a lower price, but unfortunately sometimes that is the best decision.

good luck
