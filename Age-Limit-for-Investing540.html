You must be 18 to legally have your own investment account. Not because the stock market is "gambling" (it isn't, if you know what you're doing), but because of contract law... the bank doesn't want to get screwed out of their money. Technically if you are under 18 you can void any contract you enter in to, so you could lose all your money and then tell the bank to give you your money back... they don't like that and try to avoid it.

If you have the money and knowledge but really don't want to wait until you are 18, you can set up an "in trust" account by having your parents co-sign. Your bank can provide more information.
