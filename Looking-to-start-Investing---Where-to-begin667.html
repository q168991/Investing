Take a year and read some of the weekly/monthly financial magazines, such as Money, Fortune, BusinessWeek. Watch CNBC as much as you can. Look at some of the financial websites that offer you the daily summary.
By the time you buy a book on the subject it is already outdated. Never listen to anyone's latest and greatest recommendations. Take your time studying, and remember, you will make mistakes as does everyone. Just make them on the smaller investments and you'll be in good shape.
