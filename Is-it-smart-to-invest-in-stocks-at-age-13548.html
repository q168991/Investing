I feel shares might be the way to go but like any investment, you need to understand what you are doing, protect your wealth from scams and crap advice and develop some streetwise smarts.

I would put the cash in a fixed deposit for a little while, earn a little bit of interest and build your understanding of investing in shares. You are obviously not a dill so it should not take long. Look at the ASX (Australian stock exchange) website for starters. It might be a little daunting but you need to learn the language. While you are this age, a lot of seasoned investors will be very happy to help you with advice, find one or two. Ask friends, relatives, perhaps your parents lawyer, accountant or banker can steer you in the right direction.
