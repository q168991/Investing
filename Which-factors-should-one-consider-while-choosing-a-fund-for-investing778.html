A fund over say $2 billion is extremely difficult. A lot is made of the expense ratio these days. You did not mention front end load. Avoid funds with front end loads unless they are really top notch performers. Number of stars allow you to weed out all the under performers without having to weed through a bunch of weeds. 4 and 5 star funds are a beginning.
