Hi! I have Rs.1000 per month to invest. Where to invest in bank or mutual fund?

I want good returns. I am focusing on short term investment.

If by short term you mean less than 5 years I would advise you to stay away from stocks and commodities, including gold. Internet banks will have the highest rates on money market accounts and CDs. You can check ***bankrate*** for current rates.

To take a little more risk you could buy a bond mutual fund. ***Morningstar*** will have some good recommendations on bond mutual funds. Off the top of my head Loomis Sayles Bond Return - LSBRX is a great bond fund with decent returns.
