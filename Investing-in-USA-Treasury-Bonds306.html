Investing in USA Treasury Bonds?

I wanted to know if investing in US Treasury Bonds is the right approach. If yes, how and where can I buy this bonds?

Treasury bonds are safe from default. Consequently they generally pay low yields. But in times of extreme fear and uncertainty treasury prices increase. You can buy them directly from the Treasury in increments of $100.

The returns are modest, but T-bonds are safe. You'll be repaid even if the government has to print the money. You can buy from brokerage firms (paying a commission and/or a markup), or you can buy directly from the Treasury.

They are just as high paying as long term cd's.
You can buy these through discount brokers like Charles Schwab or Fidelity Investments.
