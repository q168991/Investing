Coins have two values, the face value that the issuing government has placed on the coins and the collector's value. A investment in coins is an investment in the collector's value but the collector's value is dependent on what a buyer is willing to pay for it. In essence coins have low liquidity and hence make poor investments, certainly with respect to something like stocks.

With just a few hundred dollars, you're probably looking at something like a CD or Bonds for safe investments.
